Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you specialize in?

We specialize in both commercial and residential building design. Whether you’re looking to design a modern office space, a retail store, or a custom home, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

What is your design process?

Our design process includes several key steps: Concept, Design, Supervision, and Budget Planning. We start by understanding your needs and developing a concept, then move into detailed design, overseeing the construction, and ensuring everything stays within your budget.

How long does a typical project take?

The timeline for a project varies depending on its complexity and scope. Generally, residential projects take 3-6 months, while commercial projects can range from 6 months to a year. We’ll provide a more accurate timeline after the initial consultation.

Do you offer custom design services?

Yes, we pride ourselves on offering fully customized design services. Every project is tailored to meet the specific needs and preferences of our clients, ensuring a unique and personalized outcome.

How do you ensure the project stays on budget?

We provide thorough budget planning from the outset and closely monitor costs throughout the project. Our team works diligently to find cost-effective solutions without compromising on quality or design.
